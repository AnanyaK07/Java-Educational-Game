Tweezers:
Also known as the Forceps, this tool is used for picking something up 
without touching it or to hold something in place.

Scissors:
This tool is used to cut through bone. This is used during the Thoracic 
Dissection to cut open the ribcage.

Probe:
This tool is used to point to a part of the pig or to practice an 
incision. This tool may also be used to open the skin and soft tissue 
gently, so that no internal organs will be damaged when used.

Scalpel:
This tool is used to cut through skin and soft tissue. This is the main 
tool that is going to be used for the Abdominal Dissection.
